# Archana Reels — Design System (HyperFrames)

Brand identity for the cinematic reel engine. The composition follows this exactly.

## Mood
Sacred, warm, premium, calm. Devotional but modern — not loud, not "AI poster". Real footage carries the emotion; type and motion stay restrained and elegant.

## Palette
| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| ink | `#1A1206` | deepest shadow / vignette base |
| saffron | `#E8A33D` | primary accent, key words, brand |
| gold | `#F4D58D` | highlights, underlines, glow |
| cream | `#F6EEDD` | primary caption text on dark footage |
| maroon | `#6E1E14` | deep accent / lower-third fill |
| temple-green | `#2F5D50` | secondary accent (sparing) |

No bright pastels. No pure white (use cream). Accents sit on top of darkened real footage, never on flat gradients.

## Typography
- **Display / captions (Devanagari):** `Tiro Devanagari Hindi` — elegant, readable, devotional. Weight 400/600.
- **Latin (brand, myarchana.in):** `Inter`, weight 600.
- Caption size: 72–96px. Supporting panchang chips: 36–44px. Brand: 40px.
- `letter-spacing: 0` for Devanagari. Generous `line-height: 1.25`.

## Footage treatment
- Real Pexels portrait B-roll, full-bleed 1080×1920.
- Always darkened for legibility: radial vignette + 35–50% `#1A1206` overlay (bottom-weighted), NOT a flat full-screen gradient (H.264 banding).
- Motion: slow, cinematic — push-in / parallax-drift / gentle Ken-Burns at ≤8% scale over the segment. Never static, never fast.

## Captions (the signature)
- Word-by-word reveal synced to the Sarvam voiceover (karaoke-style), key words in `saffron`/`gold`.
- One short phrase on screen at a time, vertically centered-lower, with a soft gold underline sweep on the key word.
- Caption must fully exit before the next enters (no pile-up).

## Motion language
- Entrances: rise + fade (`power3.out`), 0.5–0.7s, staggered.
- Transitions between segments: soft crossfade / light-leak wash — never hard cuts.
- A faint floating-particle / incense-smoke layer at low opacity for life.

## Brand lockup
- Bottom: small saffron diya SVG mark + `myarchana.in` in Inter 600 cream. Subtle, never shouty.
- Final segment only: brand rises center with a gold glow.

## What NOT to do
- No emojis (themed SVG only).
- No flat full-screen gradients on dark footage (banding).
- No pure white text. No bright blue/pastel accents.
- No static text cards (the old approach — banned).
- No reading the panchang table aloud — facts are supporting visuals only.
